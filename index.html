About us

Happy Hoomens is a blockchain gaming NFT project that seeks to bridge the education gap between web2 and web3 gaming communities and charities through fun and entertaining learning focused initiatives. A percent of every genesis NFT sale will be going to one or multiple charities, as well as token buy backs of the Happy Hoomens token, TRTS, which we will also be donating to various non-profit organizations.
